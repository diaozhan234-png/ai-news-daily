Compositional Meta-Learning for Mitigating Task Heterogeneity in Physics-Informed Neural Networks
arXiv:2604.26999v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) approximate solutions of partial differential equations (PDEs) by embedding physical laws into the loss function. In parameterized PDE families, variations in coefficients or boundary/initial conditions define distinct tasks. This makes training individual PINNs for each task computationally prohibitive, while cross-task transfer can be sensitive to task heterogeneity. While meta-learning can reduce retraining cost, existing methods often rely on a single global initialization and may suffer from negative transfer, particularly under feature-scarce coordinate inputs and limited training-task availability. We propose the Learning-Affinity Adaptive Modular Physics-Informed Neural Network (LAM-PINN), a compositional framework that leverages task-specific learning dynamics. LAM-PINN combines PDE parameters with learning-affinity metrics from brief transfer sessions to construct a task representation and cluster tasks even with coordinate-only inputs. It decomposes the model into cluster-specialized subnetworks and a shared meta network, and learns routing weights to selectively reuse modules instead of relying on a single global initialization. Across three PDE benchmarks, LAM-PINN achieves an average 19.7-fold reduction in mean squared error (MSE) on unseen tasks using only 10% of the training iterations required by conventional PINNs. These results indicate its effectiveness for generalization to unseen configurations within bounded design spaces of parameterized PDE families in resource-constrained engineering settings.

减轻物理信息神经网络任务异质性的组合元学习
arXiv:2604.26999v1宣布类型：新摘要：通过将物理定律嵌入损失函数，利用物理信息神经网络（PINN）近似求解偏微分方程（PDE）。在参数化PDE族中，系数或边界/初始条件的变化定义了不同的任务。这使得为每个任务训练单个PINN在计算上变得困难，而跨任务转移可能对任务异构性敏感。虽然元学习可以降低再训练成本，但现有方法通常依赖于单一的全局初始化，可能会出现负迁移，特别是在特征稀缺的坐标输入和有限的训练任务可用性下。我们提出了学习亲和力自适应模块化物理知情神经网络（LAM-PINN），这是一个利用特定任务学习动态的组合框架。LAM-PINN将PDE参数与从简短转移会话中学习的亲和力度量相结合，以构建任务表示，并对仅使用坐标输入的任务进行聚类。它将模型分解为集群专用子网络和共享元网络，并学习路由权重以选择性地重用模块，而不是依赖于单个全局初始化。在三个PDE基准测试中，LAM-PINN仅使用传统PINN所需训练迭代的10%，在看不见的任务上平均将均方误差（MSE）降低了19.7倍。这些结果表明，在资源受限的工程环境中，它对参数化PDE族的有界设计空间内的不可见配置的泛化是有效的。

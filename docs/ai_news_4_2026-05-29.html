Glean’s top line crosses $300M as AI budget-cutting becomes its major selling point
Glean, a company often described as the Google for enterprise, said it has reached $300 million in annual recurring revenue (ARR), a three-fold increase from the $100 million milestone it reached just 15 months ago. While many AI startups are growing at a blistering pace, Glean’s progress is particularly remarkable. After years of essentially being the only player in the category, the seven-year-old startup is accelerating its growth as tech giants enter the enterprise AI search market with rival products. “The first four or five years of our existence, we had no competition,” Glean CEO Arvind Jain told TechCrunch. “Given how important search is to make AI work in the enterprise, every single company in the world wants to be in this space.” Tech heavyweights building Glean-like tools include Google, Microsoft, OpenAI, Anthropic, Salesforce, and Atlassian. Jain maintains that there’s value in being a first mover in the space, but that it’s also equally important to offer a better product. What Glean does better than its competition, according to Jain, comes down to the deep understanding that its AI tools have of customers’ business needs. Glean’s AI achieves this knowledge — a concept captured by the new, popular term “ context graph ” — by connecting to and learning from enterprises’ internal software systems. Jain claims that Glean’s context graph also helps enterprises cut AI computing costs. “If you connect your AI to Glean, it gives you all the information that you need to do your work, and that results in AI consuming far fewer tokens compared to if you unleash AI onto your systems directly,” Jain said. That’s because with Glean, AI ends up performing fewer operations, he added. At a time when many companies are blowing through their AI budgets, those token cost savings have become a major selling point for the company. “One of the things you know our customers really like about Glean is the fact that we can reduce your AI bill significantly,” he said. The company, which was last valued at $7.2 billion when it raised a $150 million Series F last June, offers various pricing structures to its customers, which include Databricks, Reddit, Pinterest, and Samsung. According to Jain, Glean offers both a consumption-based model, where clients pay per use, and a hybrid model that combines a fixed monthly fee for active users with separate usage fees for model consumption. Glean is definitely not the first company to do this, but it’s worth pointing out that the company’s $300 million milestone cannot be fully described as traditional ARR, because a consumption model by definition doesn’t have a strictly recurring component. Pure consumption pricing models depend on fluctuating user activity rather than predictable subscription renewals, therefore a portion of Glean’s topline is more accurately described as an annualized revenue run rate. Glean did not immediately respond to a request for comment; this post will be updated if the company replies.

随着人工智能预算削减成为其主要卖点，Glean的收入突破3亿美元
Glean，一家经常被称为企业版谷歌的公司，表示其年度经常性收入（ARR）已达到3亿美元，比15个月前达到的1亿美元里程碑增长了三倍。尽管许多人工智能初创公司正以惊人的速度发展，但Glean的进步尤其显著。在多年来基本上是该类别中唯一的参与者之后，这家成立七年的初创公司正在加速增长，因为科技巨头正以竞争对手的产品进入企业人工智能搜索市场。Glean首席执行官Arvind Jain告诉TechCrunch：“在我们成立的前四五年，我们没有竞争对手。”。“鉴于搜索对于让人工智能在企业中发挥作用有多重要，世界上每一家公司都希望进入这个领域。”构建类似Glean工具的技术巨头包括谷歌、微软、OpenAI、Anthropic、Salesforce和Atlassian。Jain认为，成为该领域的先行者是有价值的，但提供更好的产品同样重要。Jain表示，Glean比竞争对手做得更好的原因在于其人工智能工具对客户业务需求的深刻理解。Glean的人工智能通过连接到企业内部软件系统并从中学习来实现这一知识——这是一个新的流行术语“上下文图”所捕捉到的概念。Jain声称，Glean的上下文图也有助于企业降低人工智能计算成本。Jain说：“如果你将人工智能连接到Glean，它会为你提供完成工作所需的所有信息，与直接将人工智能释放到你的系统上相比，这会导致人工智能消耗更少的代币。”。他补充道，这是因为有了Glean，人工智能最终执行的操作更少。在许多公司都在挥霍人工智能预算的时候，这些象征性的成本节约已经成为该公司的主要卖点。他说：“你知道我们的客户非常喜欢Glean的一点是，我们可以大大减少你的人工智能账单。”。该公司上一次估值为72亿美元，是在去年6月筹集1.5亿美元的F轮融资时，为其客户提供了各种定价结构，其中包括copula、Reddit、Pinterest和三星。据Jain介绍，Glean提供了一种基于消费的模式，客户按次付费，以及一种混合模式，该模式将活跃用户的固定月费与模型消费的单独使用费相结合。Glean绝对不是第一家这样做的公司，但值得指出的是，该公司的3亿美元里程碑不能完全被描述为传统的ARR，因为从定义上讲，消费模式没有严格的重复性成分。纯消费定价模型依赖于波动的用户活动，而不是可预测的订阅续订，因此Glean的部分收入更准确地描述为年化收入运行率。Glean没有立即回应置评请求；如果公司回复，这篇文章将会更新。

OpenKedge: Governing Agentic Mutation with Execution-Bound Safety and Evidence Chains
arXiv:2604.08601v1 Announce Type: new Abstract: The rise of autonomous AI agents exposes a fundamental flaw in API-centric architectures: probabilistic systems directly execute state mutations without sufficient context, coordination, or safety guarantees. We introduce OpenKedge, a protocol that redefines mutation as a governed process rather than an immediate consequence of API invocation. OpenKedge requires actors to submit declarative intent proposals, which are evaluated against deterministically derived system state, temporal signals, and policy constraints prior to execution. Approved intents are compiled into execution contracts that strictly bound permitted actions, resource scope, and time, and are enforced via ephemeral, task-oriented identities. This shifts safety from reactive filtering to preventative, execution-bound enforcement. Crucially, OpenKedge introduces an Intent-to-Execution Evidence Chain (IEEC), which cryptographically links intent, context, policy decisions, execution bounds, and outcomes into a unified lineage. This transforms mutation into a verifiable and reconstructable process, enabling deterministic auditability and reasoning about system behavior. We evaluate OpenKedge across multi-agent conflict scenarios and cloud infrastructure mutations. Results show that the protocol deterministically arbitrates competing intents and cages unsafe execution while maintaining high throughput, establishing a principled foundation for safely operating agentic systems at scale.

OpenKedge：具有执行约束安全和证据链的治理机构突变
arXiv:2604.08601v1发布类型：新摘要：自主人工智能代理的兴起暴露了以API为中心的架构中的一个根本缺陷：概率系统在没有足够的上下文、协调或安全保证的情况下直接执行状态突变。我们引入了OpenKedge，这是一个协议，它将突变重新定义为受管理的过程，而不是API调用的直接结果。OpenKedge要求参与者提交声明性意图建议，在执行之前根据确定性导出的系统状态、时间信号和策略约束对其进行评估。批准的意图被编译成执行契约，严格约束允许的操作、资源范围和时间，并通过短暂的、面向任务的身份来执行。这将安全从被动过滤转变为预防性、执行约束的执行。至关重要的是，OpenKedge引入了意图执行证据链（IEEC），该链将意图、上下文、政策决策、执行边界和结果加密地链接到一个统一的谱系中。这将突变转化为可验证和可重建的过程，实现了对系统行为的确定性审计和推理。我们在多代理冲突场景和云基础设施突变中评估OpenKedge。结果表明，该协议在保持高吞吐量的同时，确定性地仲裁了竞争意图并限制了不安全的执行，为大规模安全操作代理系统奠定了原则基础。

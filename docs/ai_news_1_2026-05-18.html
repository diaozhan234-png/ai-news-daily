Why trust is a big question at the Elon Musk-OpenAI trial

Kirsten noted that Musk has made plenty of misleading statements of his own , and that trust isn’t just an issue for Altman. “This is a fundamental question [for] a lot of tech journalists, policymakers, and more and more consumers, about all the AI labs,” she said. “It’s really come down to trust, because we don’t have the insight, necessarily — these are all privately held companies, there’s a lot behind the veil still.” Keep reading for a preview of our conversation, edited for length and clarity.

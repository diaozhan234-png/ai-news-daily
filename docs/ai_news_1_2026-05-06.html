SAP bets $1.16B on 18-month-old German AI lab and says yes to NemoClaw
By OpenAI COO’s own admission last February, “we have not yet really seen AI penetrate enterprise business processes.” But for enterprise software giant SAP, whose stock has dropped significantly in 2026 in part from the “SaaSpocalypse,” the issue is still front and center. On Monday, the European heavyweight announced its intention to acquire German AI startup Prior Labs for an undisclosed amount. Pending regulatory approval, SAP plans to invest €1 billion (approximately $1.16 billion) into the business over the next four years to grow it into an AI lab focused on structured data — the tables and databases where enterprise information typically sits. SAP declined to disclose how much it spent on the acquisition itself, but sources told Pathfounders that this was a healthy exit: an “almost all cash” deal, with well over half a billion dollars in cash up front for the startup’s founders — Frank Hutter, Noah Hollmann, and Sauraj Gambhir. The trio co-founded Prior Labs just 18 months ago with a focus on tabular foundation models (TFMs) — AI models that can make predictions from data that sits in tables and databases. This is potentially a better fit for enterprises than language models. It is certainly a better fit for SAP, whose widely used software products for accounting, HR, procurement and expense management rely on its database. However, Germany’s most valuable company also seems be playing defense as the tech industry marches toward agentic AI. While it works to create its own AI lab, the company has blocked OpenClaw and any other agent tech that it has not explicitly authorized, The Information was first to spot. In response to a request for comment, SAP’s press department referred TechCrunch to the company’s latest API policy , which does say that SAP “prohibits” AI agents from accessing its products through its API except for those that are “SAP-endorsed architectures.” Authorized architectures of course include SAP’s own offering, Joule Agents , still in beta, which lets customers create their own agents. Nvidia also announced in March that SAP’s Joule supports Nvidia’s Agent Toolkit , which is software for managing agents. This toolkit is the foundation for Nvidia’s enterprise-ready, security-focused OpenClaw competitor, NemoClaw. Hence SAP customers will be authorized to use NemoClaw agents. For a giant incumbent player like SAP, AI is both a threat and an opportunity. “It’s all about how quickly [we can] as SAP actually also embark [on] these technologies in our R&D portfolio to keep the relative economies of scale advantage,” CFO Dominik Asam told CNBC in January. SAP hasn’t been sitting on its hands. The German company invested in generative AI companies that develop language models large and small: In 2023, it backed OpenAI rival Anthropic — as well as Aleph Alpha and Cohere, which now intend to merge to form “a global AI powerhouse.” It had also developed SAP-RPT-1 , a relational pretrained transformer model. “Early on, SAP recognized that the greatest untapped opportunity in enterprise AI wasn’t large language models; it was AI built for the structured data that runs the world’s businesses,” SAP CTO Philipp Herzig declared in a statement. But Prior Labs’ acquisition is a significant shortcut in that direction. Its TabPFN model series has experienced a lot of traction among developers. In a blog post on the deal, the startup’s founders said that its open source models have been downloaded over three million times. In a press release, SAP promised that Prior Labs will maintain the open source versions: “The lab will operate as an independent unit to ensure research velocity while SAP provides long-term investment and a direct path to productization across the SAP portfolio with SAP AI Core and SAP Business Data Cloud as well as the agentic layer with Joule.” SAP and the startup headquartered in Freiburg, Germany, hope that this investment will lead to TFMs that can grab data in the tables where it lives, combine that with language, reasoning, and domain knowledge. More than that, they hope that Prior Labs, with this “massive boost” from SAP, can become a new “globally-leading frontier AI lab for structured data — in Europe, in the open,” founder and CEO Frank Hutter celebrated in a post on X . In February 2025, the startup had previously raised some $9.3 million in a pre-seed funding round led by Balderton Capital — more than competitor Neuralk-AI , but a lot less than Fundamental , which emerged out of stealth with a $255 million Series A in February.

SAP对成立18个月的德国人工智能实验室下注11.6亿美元，并对NemoClaw表示同意
OpenAI首席运营官去年2月承认，“我们还没有真正看到人工智能渗透到企业业务流程中。”但对于企业软件巨头SAP来说，这个问题仍然是首要问题，其股价在2026年因“SaaSpocalypse”而大幅下跌。周一，这家欧洲重量级企业宣布有意收购德国人工智能初创公司Prior Labs，收购金额不详。在获得监管部门批准之前，SAP计划在未来四年内向该业务投资10亿欧元（约合11.6亿美元），将其发展成为一个专注于结构化数据（企业信息通常所在的表和数据库）的人工智能实验室。SAP拒绝透露其在收购本身上花费了多少，但消息人士告诉Pathfounders，这是一个健康的退出：一项“几乎全现金”的交易，为初创公司的创始人Frank Hutter、Noah Hollmann和Sauraj Gambhir预付了5亿多美元的现金。这三人在18个月前共同创立了Prior Labs，专注于表格基础模型（TFM）——可以从表格和数据库中的数据进行预测的人工智能模型。这可能比语言模型更适合企业。它当然更适合SAP，SAP广泛使用的会计、人力资源、采购和费用管理软件产品都依赖于其数据库。然而，随着科技行业向代理人工智能迈进，这家德国最有价值的公司似乎也在进行防御。虽然该公司正在努力创建自己的人工智能实验室，但该公司已经阻止了OpenClaw和任何其他未经明确授权的代理技术，the Information是第一个发现的。在回应置评请求时，SAP新闻部将TechCrunch介绍给了该公司最新的API政策，该政策确实规定，SAP“禁止”人工智能代理通过其API访问其产品，但“支持SAP的架构”除外。当然，授权架构包括SAP自己的产品Joule agents，该产品仍处于测试版，允许客户创建自己的代理。Nvidia还在3月份宣布，SAP的Joule支持Nvidia的Agent Toolkit，这是一款用于管理代理的软件。该工具包是Nvidia面向企业、以安全为重点的OpenClaw竞争对手NemoClaw的基础。因此，SAP客户将被授权使用NemoClaw代理。对于像SAP这样的巨头来说，人工智能既是威胁，也是机遇。首席财务官Dominik Asam在1月份告诉CNBC：“这一切都取决于（我们）能多快，因为SAP实际上也开始在我们的研发组合中采用这些技术，以保持相对的规模经济优势。”。SAP并没有袖手旁观。这家德国公司投资了开发大大小小语言模型的生成式人工智能公司：2023年，它支持了OpenAI的竞争对手Anthropic，以及Aleph Alpha和Cohere，他们现在打算合并形成“全球人工智能巨头”。它还开发了SAP-RPT-1，一种关系预训练转换模型。SAP首席技术官Philipp Herzig在一份声明中表示：“早期，SAP认识到企业人工智能最大的未开发机会不是大型语言模型；而是为运行世界业务的结构化数据构建的人工智能。”。但Prior Labs的收购是朝着这个方向迈出的一条重要捷径。其TabPFN型号系列在开发人员中获得了很大的吸引力。在一篇关于这笔交易的博客文章中，这家初创公司的创始人表示，其开源模型已被下载超过300万次。在一份新闻稿中，SAP承诺Prior Labs将保持开源版本：“该实验室将作为一个独立的单位运营，以确保研究速度，而SAP将提供长期投资，并通过SAP AI Core和SAP Business Data Cloud以及Joule的代理层在SAP产品组合中实现产品化。”SAP和总部位于德国弗莱堡的初创公司希望这项投资将导致TFM能够从其所在的表中获取数据，并将其与语言、推理和领域知识相结合。更重要的是，他们希望Prior Labs在SAP的“巨大推动”下，能够成为一个新的“全球领先的结构化数据前沿人工智能实验室——在欧洲开放”，创始人兼首席执行官Frank Hutter在X上的一篇帖子中表示。2025年2月，这家初创公司之前在Balderton Capital领导的种子轮融资中筹集了约930万美元，超过了竞争对手Neuwalk AI，但远低于Fundamental，后者在2月份以2.55亿美元的a轮融资脱颖而出。

Anthropic raises Claude Code usage limits, credits new deal with SpaceX
SAN FRANCISCO—At its Code with Claude developer conference on Wednesday, Anthropic announced a deal with SpaceX to utilize the entire compute capacity of the latter’s data center in Memphis, Tennessee. On stage at the conference, CEO Dario Amodei said the deal was intended to increase usage limits for Anthropic’s Pro and Max plan subscribers. The announcement was accompanied by an increase in those usage limits; Anthropic doubled Claude Code’s five-hour window limits for Pro and Max subscribers, removed the peak-hours limit reduction on Claude Code for those same accounts, and raised API limits for its Opus model. The table below outlining the Opus changes was shared in the company’s blog post on the topic. Anthropic claims the deal gives the company access to more than 300 megawatts of new compute capacity. For its part, SpaceX focused its announcement on the capability of the Colossus 1 supercomputer that’s at the center of the deal. “Colossus 1 features over 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs, including dense deployments of H100, H200, and next-generation GB200 accelerators,” SpaceX wrote . Additionally, Anthropic “expressed interest” in working with SpaceX to build up “multiple gigawatts” of orbital compute capacity, tying into a recent (but unproven ) focus on exploring orbital data centers as an answer to the problem that “compute required to train and operate the next generation of these systems is outpacing what terrestrial power, land, and cooling can deliver on the timelines that matter.” The deal might be surprising to those who have followed Musk’s recent public comments—he was, until now, critical of Anthropic. For example, in February, he declared on X that “Anthropic hates Western Civilization,” while sharing a false tweet from Trump administration official Emil Michael about Anthropic’s practices with its constitution for Claude. The tune changed with the deal—or into the lead-up to it, as Musk tells it. “I spent a lot of time last week with senior members of the Anthropic team to understand what they do to ensure Claude is good for humanity and was impressed,” Musk tweeted on Wednesday. “No one set off my evil detector.” Anthropic has seen a significant increase in demand for Claude Code and other products related to its models over the past few months. The increase has been driven partly by users moving away from OpenAI after controversy over its agreements with the United States military, increasing adoption of Claude Code in professional software development organizations, and a user behavior (and product) shift away from single-agent, chat-based tasks to more demanding multi-agent workflows. The company has made controversial moves lately to address demand outpacing available compute capacity, amid outages and other problems. That included introducing new usage limits during peak hours, and even a short-lived and very limited trial, apparently testing the notion of removing Claude Code from the $20/month Pro plan. Vocal frustration with usage limits has been a staple of Hacker News, Reddit, X, and other platforms where software developers congregate. Many developers are using these models and tools, and they’re frustrated that they can’t use them more. Last month, Anthropic reportedly signed massive deals with Microsoft, Google , Amazon , Nvidia, and more to scale up its access to compute infrastructure. The company credits those, along with this SpaceX deal, for its ability to raise limits, though gains from some of them will take time to materialize.

Anthropic提高了Claude Code的使用限制，归功于与SpaceX的新协议
旧金山-在周三举行的Code with Claude开发者大会上，Anthropic宣布与SpaceX达成协议，利用后者位于田纳西州孟菲斯的数据中心的全部计算能力。在会议的舞台上，首席执行官Dario Amodei表示，该协议旨在提高Anthropic Pro和Max计划用户的使用限制。该公告同时提高了这些使用限制；Anthropic将Claude Code对Pro和Max用户的五小时窗口限制增加了一倍，取消了Claude Code对这些账户的高峰时间限制，并提高了其Opus模型的API限制。下表概述了Opus的变化，并在该公司关于该主题的博客文章中分享。Anthropic声称，这笔交易使该公司能够获得超过300兆瓦的新计算能力。就SpaceX而言，其宣布的重点是作为交易核心的Colossus 1超级计算机的能力。“巨像1配备了超过22万个NVIDIA GPU，包括密集部署的H100、H200和下一代GB200加速器，”SpaceX写道。此外，Anthropic“表示有兴趣”与SpaceX合作建立“多吉瓦”的轨道计算能力，这与最近（但未经证实）对探索轨道数据中心的关注相结合，以解决“训练和运行下一代这些系统所需的计算速度超过了地面电力、土地和冷却在重要时间内所能提供的速度”的问题。这笔交易可能会让那些关注马斯克最近公开评论的人感到惊讶——到目前为止，他一直对Anthropics持批评态度。例如，2月，他在X上宣布“Anthropic憎恨西方文明”，同时分享了特朗普政府官员埃米尔·迈克尔关于Anthropics对克劳德宪法的做法的虚假推文。正如马斯克告诉的那样，随着交易的进行，气氛发生了变化。马斯克周三在推特上写道：“上周我花了很多时间与人类团队的高级成员在一起，了解他们为确保克劳德对人类有益所做的工作，给我留下了深刻的印象。”。“没有人能触发我的邪恶探测器。”在过去的几个月里，Anthropic公司对克劳德密码和其他与其型号相关的产品的需求大幅增加。这一增长部分是由于用户在与美国军方的协议引发争议后离开OpenAI，专业软件开发组织越来越多地采用Claude Code，以及用户行为（和产品）从单一代理、基于聊天的任务转向要求更高的多代理工作流程。在停电和其他问题中，该公司最近采取了有争议的举措来解决需求超过可用计算能力的问题。这包括在高峰时段引入新的使用限制，甚至是一个短暂且非常有限的试验，显然是在测试从每月20美元的Pro计划中删除Claude Code的想法。对使用限制的不满一直是Hacker News、Reddit、X和其他软件开发人员聚集的平台的主要内容。许多开发人员正在使用这些模型和工具，他们对不能更多地使用它们感到沮丧。据报道，上个月，Anthropic与微软、谷歌、亚马逊、英伟达等签署了大规模协议，以扩大其对计算基础设施的访问。该公司将这些以及与SpaceX的这笔交易归功于其提高限额的能力，尽管其中一些收益需要时间才能实现。

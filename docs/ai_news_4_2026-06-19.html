Unlocking UK house-building with AI-accelerated planning
Around the world, Governments are exploring how AI can deliver better public services, faster. The UK is working to build 1.5 million new homes by 2029, but local planning authorities are often slowed down by dense paperwork and administrative backlogs. To help get Britain building, we’re partnering with the UK government to help radically shorten the time it takes to process householder planning applications. Our goal is to help officers cut application decision times by 50%, freeing up time for planners so that more homes can be built. We’re excited to see how our National Partnerships for AI , which seek to support reimagining of public services to create more resilient societies, can help Britain build faster. Following the successful launch of Extract — a tool built with Gemini by the UK government's Incubator for AI (i.AI) — to help councils turn old planning documents into clear, digital data — we’re partnering to develop a sophisticated new planning prototype for councils. Google DeepMind is working alongside the UK government, Google Cloud, Faculty, and local planning authorities in Barnet, Dorset and Camden, to co-develop an AI-powered prototype that acts as a highly skilled assistant for planning officers, handling the heavy lifting of data extraction and case analysis. Working in close partnership will enable us to build an optimized tool, suited to the unique needs and day-to-day challenges that planners contend with every day. Following early trials in Barnet, Camden and Dorset, the government plans for the new AI planning tool to be made available to all councils nationally from 2027. For a typical new planning application, officers spend hours cross-referencing policy documents, historical files and PDFs. This manual process creates a major bottleneck. It is especially challenging given householder applications account for nearly 70% of planning applications each year. By reducing the time spent on straightforward cases like loft conversions or extensions, the prototype being tested in Barnet, Camden and Dorset could help planning officers focus more on complex applications for public benefit. They review every line the tool generates, edit the reasoning and retain the authority to approve or reject an application. To ensure accountability, the prototype records its work at every step, creating a clear chain of thought and a robust audit trail for every decision to support the planning officer. The AI planning tool builds on the foundations set by Extract — which was made available to every council in England earlier this month. Extract solves a common problem: critical planning information is often locked away in unstructured PDFs. Extract is able to turn hundreds of pages of legacy planning documents into usable data in minutes. Extract has already shown great success in trials across more than 20 local planning authorities — and is expected to save the average council around 255 hours of manual work a year. Our goal is to create a more responsive and transparent planning tool that benefits everyone. By reducing the administrative burden on officers, we can accelerate the economic activity that comes with building and help more people improve their homes. As we move forward, we will continue to work together to ensure these tools help meet the evolving needs of the UK's planning ecosystem. We hope these developments will serve as a model for other governments around the world as they look to navigate the future of public service delivery.

通过人工智能加速规划解锁英国房屋建筑
在世界各地，政府正在探索人工智能如何更快地提供更好的公共服务。英国正在努力到2029年建造150万套新住房，但当地规划部门往往因繁重的文书工作和行政积压而放慢速度。为了帮助英国建设，我们正在与英国政府合作，帮助从根本上缩短处理房主规划申请的时间。我们的目标是帮助官员将申请决策时间缩短50%，为规划者腾出时间，以便建造更多的房屋。我们很高兴看到我们的国家人工智能伙伴关系如何帮助英国更快地建设，该伙伴关系旨在支持重新构想公共服务，以创造更具弹性的社会。在成功推出Extract（英国政府人工智能孵化器（i.AI）与Gemini共同开发的工具）以帮助议会将旧的规划文件转化为清晰的数字数据之后，我们正在合作为议会开发一个复杂的新规划原型。谷歌DeepMind正在与英国政府、谷歌云、学院以及巴内特、多塞特和卡姆登的当地规划部门合作，共同开发一种人工智能原型，作为规划人员的高技能助手，处理繁重的数据提取和案例分析工作。密切合作将使我们能够构建一个优化的工具，以适应规划者每天面临的独特需求和日常挑战。在巴内特、卡姆登和多塞特进行了早期试验后，政府计划从2027年开始向全国所有议会提供新的人工智能规划工具。对于一个典型的新规划应用程序，官员们会花几个小时交叉引用政策文件、历史文件和PDF。这个手动过程造成了一个主要的瓶颈。考虑到房主申请每年占规划申请的近70%，这尤其具有挑战性。通过减少在阁楼改造或扩建等简单案例上花费的时间，在巴内特、卡姆登和多塞特测试的原型可以帮助规划人员更专注于复杂的公共利益应用。人工智能规划工具将通过人工智能简化日常任务：至关重要的是，规划官员作为最终决策者仍然拥有完全的控制权。他们审查工具生成的每一行，编辑推理，并保留批准或拒绝申请的权限。为确保问责制，原型记录了每一步的工作，为每一个决策创建了清晰的思路和强有力的审计跟踪，以支持规划官员。人工智能规划工具建立在Extract建立的基础上，Extract本月早些时候向英国的每个议会提供了该工具。Extract解决了一个常见问题：关键的规划信息通常被锁定在非结构化的PDF中。Extract能够在几分钟内将数百页的遗留规划文档转化为可用数据。Extract已经在20多个地方规划部门的试验中取得了巨大成功，预计每年将为议会节省约255小时的体力劳动。我们的目标是创建一个更具响应性和透明度的规划工具，使每个人都能受益。通过减轻官员的行政负担，我们可以加快建筑带来的经济活动，帮助更多人改善他们的住房。随着我们向前迈进，我们将继续共同努力，确保这些工具有助于满足英国规划生态系统不断变化的需求。我们希望这些发展将成为世界各地其他政府在探索公共服务交付未来时的典范。
